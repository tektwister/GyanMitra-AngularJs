About Us

Mepco Schlenk Engineering College (Autonomous), Sivakasi

Our College is one of the reputed educational Institutions, conferred the Autonomous Status by UGC, New Delhi and affiliated to Anna University. All the seven UG programmes of our college are accredited by NBA, New Delhi under TIER-I Category. Also, our college has secured the prestigious Bharadiya Vidya Bhavan National Award for an Engineering College having the best overall performance for the year 1998 and 2005, awarded by ISTE. We have also received the ISO 9001:2015 certificates from M\s. DNV, Netherlands. Our college was already approved as “Scientific and Industrial Research Organization (SIRO)” by Ministry of Science & Technology. Our college won the 2008 IMV Ramakrishna Bajaj National Quality Trophy. Our college got 15th position in the Ranking of Top Engineering Colleges of Eminence at National level category in the CSR-GHRDC Engineering Colleges survey by Competition Success Review magazine, July 2015. Our College secured the 30th Position among India's TOP Professional Colleges by Outlook Survey, June 2017. Our College secured the 73rd position in NIRF ranking. It is my pleasure to convey that our college is organizing a two days National Level Techno Management Symposium, GYAN MITRA’19 during 8th - 9th February 2019. It is a colloquium of 8 technical domains including Bio-Tech, Civil, Computer Science, ECE, EEE, Mechanical, MCA and Maths & Science, collectively organized by 22 Societies functioning in our college. During GYAN MITRA’19, 21 Workshops on recent technologies, 72 Technical Events and 13 Paper Presentations will be conducted. It will be a meeting point of students and professionals from various parts of the country. GYAN MITRA organized in the year 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2018 are well recognized by the student participants and they won prizes worth Rs. 1.5 lakhs in every year.
