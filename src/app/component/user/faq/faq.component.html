Frequently Asked Questions


*HOW TO REGISTER AN ACCOUNT?

Follow the steps below:

  1. Click on "Register" in Homepage or on the top right corner
  2. Enter the registration details on the form
  3. After entering the details, click "Register". An Activation mail will be sent to the registered mail
  4. Login into your email-account and click the link sent from Gyanmitra'19
  5. Upon clicking the link, you will be redirected to the gyanmitra homepage and your account is registered. You can login and view/register for events and workshops
*How to register for a event?

Follow the steps below

  1. Login into your account and Click "Events" in the sidebar
  2. Select your department
  3. If it is Individual Event then click Register Now and your registration will be noted
  4. If it is Team Event then click Register Team
  5. Enter the team name and type the name of the all the members
  6. Only the team leader must add the members during the registration of the event, all the members will be registered automatically.
  7. You will receive a notification on the top of the screen if the registration is successful
*How to register for a workshop?

Follow the steps below

  1. Login into your account and Click "workshop" in the sidebar
  2. Select your department and clickRegister Now
  3. You will receive a notification on the top of the screen if the registration is successful
*HOW TO MAKE PAYMENT?

Follow the steps below

  1. Login into your account and Click "Cart" in the sidebar
  2. Select Confirm Carton the top right corner
    YOU CANNOT REGISTER ANY EVENTS/WORKSHOPS AFTER CONFIRMING CART
  3. You will receive a notification that your cart is confirmed. Now select any mode of payment
    • Online Payment
    • Through DD

  4. Payment Through DD :You will receive instructions to make payment as below
  5. Online Payment :Click Pay and you will be taken to the online payment portal
*HOW TO APPLY FOR ACCOMMODATION?

Follow the steps below

  1. Login into your account and Click "Accomodation" in the sidebar
  2. Select the number of days of stay
  3. Once the admin approves your accommodation request, you can pay through any mode of payment
    • Online Payment
    • Through DD
